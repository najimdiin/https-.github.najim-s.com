Careers in Africa

 
   

Careers in Africa has been recruiting excellence in Africa for 15 years, and is justly renowned for the Careers in Africa Recruitment Summits, a world-leading initiative bringing together up to 50 of the continent’s leading employers with up to 1,000 high-calibre and high-potential professionals.

We have supported more than 550 employers in recruiting across 54 markets, and the brand has grown steadily since its inception to demonstrate and strong track record across Anglophone, Francophone and Lusophone Africa. Services offered through Careers in Africa include:

  • Online Recruitment
  • Recruitment Campaigns
  • Recruitment Summits
  • Employee and Talent Pool Insight
  • Talent Hackathons
